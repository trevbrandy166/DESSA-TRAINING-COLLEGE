About Dessa Training College

Building careers in Accounting and Technology since 2024

Our Story

The Path to Knowledge Starts Here

Founded in 2024, Dessa Training College began with a simple mission: to provide quality, accessible education in accounting and technology to students across Kenya. What started as a small computer training center has grown into one of the region's most respected institutions for professional certifications.

Our name "Dessa" embodies our commitment to Determination, Excellence, Success, Skills, and Achievement — the five pillars that guide everything we do.

Today, we serve over 1,000 students annually, offering both online and physical classes that fit the schedules of working professionals, recent graduates, and career changers alike.
